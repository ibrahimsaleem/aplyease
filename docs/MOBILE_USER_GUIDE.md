## Gestures & Interactions

### Pull to Refresh
1. Scroll to the top of the application list
2. Pull down with your finger
3. Keep pulling until you see the refresh icon
4. Release to refresh
5. Data updates automatically

### Tap Interactions
- **Single tap** on a card to expand/collapse details
- **Tap buttons** to perform actions
- **Tap links** to open job postings or resumes in new tabs

### Scrolling
- **Scroll vertically** through your application cards

## Troubleshooting

### Cards Not Loading?
1. Check your internet connection
2. Pull down to refresh
3. Try logging out and back in

### Search Not Working?
1. Make sure you tapped the Search button
2. Type at least 2-3 characters
3. Tap Search or press Enter

### Filters Not Applying?
1. Make sure you tapped "Apply Filters"
2. Check if any applications match your filters
3. Try resetting filters and applying again

### Bottom Navigation Not Visible?
1. Scroll to see it (it's always at the bottom)
2. It may be hidden by your keyboard - close keyboard to see it
3. Refresh the page if it's missing
